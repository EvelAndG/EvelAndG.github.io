Ghost of Tsushima

Developed by Sucker Punch and published by Sony

This game follows the journey of Jin Sakai during the first Mongol invasion of Japan. The honorable samurai, Jin, must work towards freeing his homeland from Mongolian invaders at the same time he wrestles with his own code of honor.

Score: Absolute Masterpiece 10/10

Marvel's Avengers Game

Developed by Crystal Dynamics and Published by Square Enix

The Avengers game is a classic third person action-brawler with a more modern spin on combat including characters from the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Includes a full story campaign which has sections to play as each of the starter heroes. Future DLC has been announced as free, with two dlc additions including characters have already been added.
