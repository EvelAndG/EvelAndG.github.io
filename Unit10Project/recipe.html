Chili Recipe
Ingredient Name Amount Picture
Ground Beef 1 lb.
Red, Black, Pinto, or Kidney Beans 2 cans or 32 oz.
Chili Seasoning Mix 1 packet or 1.25 oz.
Tomato Paste One 6 oz. can
Water 1 or two cups
NO GREEN PEPPER OR ONION -EWW!!!
  • -Cook ground beef in skillet until brown
  • -Add beans, water and chili mix to the cooked meat in large pot
  • -Simmer on low heat for 4-6 hours while stirring regularly
  • -Chili should maintain a 'bubble' and NOT a boil for a minumum of one hour at least, the longer the better

  • -Ready to eat!
  • I suggest saltine crackers and a peanut butter sandwich to dip in your chili
